The experience
A house shaped by heat and shadow.

You move through warm, dark rooms at your own pace. Heat, steam, cold and quiet form a calm passage that lets the rest of the week fall away.

Heat Room

Dry heat, deep timber and still air. The room is tuned for steady warmth, held in deep timber and stillness.

Steam Room

Soft warmth and low light. Steam gathers gently so breath can slow and the room eases you into stillness.

Cold Well

Stone, shadow and cold water held in stillness. Close enough to the heat that contrast feels natural, not forced.

Quiet Room

Greenery in darkness and warm pockets of light. A silent room for sitting, closing your eyes, or simply being still.

Outdoors

Outside, the Ember Deck brings warm water, timber and fire together. In winter, the air is sharp while the tubs glow. In summer, evenings stretch out and the heat line finishes beneath open sky.

Membership
A key held for your return.

Membership is how you keep this room apart from the rest of your life. Numbers stay limited and bookings are controlled so the house stays warm, quiet and calm.

Room Key

Your place within the house. A calm way to begin a pattern of return.

Inner Key

Quieter access and deeper parts of the house for people who want more time in the warm and dark.

Seventh Key

The rarest key. A deeper room, priority booking and first access to certain evenings and outdoor events.

How it works

You choose a key that matches how you like to visit. You book times in advance through a simple, quiet app. We keep numbers low so you never arrive to a crowd, and you return whenever you need a quieter room.

Why we keep numbers low

Calm is only predictable when it is protected. That means holding the line on memberships, bookings and noise, even when demand is high.

Founding members

Before opening, a small number of founding memberships will be available. Founding members will see the finished house first, hold quieter rates for as long as they stay, and be invited to the first evenings in the space.

The house
A sequence of thresholds.

Light falls, sound softens and the outside world drops away in stages. The building itself is part of the experience.

The journey

You step through warm shadow, leave your things behind and move gradually through heat, steam, cold and quiet.

Each doorway brings you further from noise and closer to your own breath. The house is organised as a simple passage from outside to inner quiet.

Materials

  • Natural plaster and smoked oak.
  • Stone under bare feet and warm, indirect light.
  • Linen and wool where the body meets fabric.
  • Plants chosen for shape and shadow, not abundance.

Questions
What people ask.

Is this a gym?

No. There are no machines or classes. The house is built entirely around heat, steam, cold and quiet.

Can I visit without being a member?

Occasionally. A small number of day passes will be offered at quieter times so people can experience the house before deciding to join. These will always be limited.

Is it adults-only?

Yes. The rooms are designed around adult calm. There are no family swim times or children’s areas.

Is it suitable if I am new to saunas or cold water?

Yes. Staff can introduce the rooms gently and everything is optional. You move at your own pace.

How often should I come?

Many people find a weekly pattern helpful, and some visit more often when they need a quieter room. There are no attendance targets.

What should I bring?

We will provide robes, towels and basic toiletries. You just need a swimming costume. Some people read, many simply close their eyes.
